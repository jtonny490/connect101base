# Problem & Solution

## The Problem

Traditional resumes are difficult to verify.

People can claim:

* Previous employment
* Freelance projects
* Consulting experience
* Client work

without proof.

Verification requires:

* Phone calls
* Emails
* References
* Background checks

This process is slow, expensive, and unreliable.

At the same time, reputation is trapped inside platforms.

Examples:

* Fiverr reviews stay on Fiverr.
* Upwork reviews stay on Upwork.
* LinkedIn recommendations stay on LinkedIn.

Users do not own their professional reputation.

## Existing Solutions

### Traditional CVs

Problems:

* Self-reported
* Easy to fake
* Static documents
* Require manual verification

### Freelance Platforms

Problems:

* Platform lock-in
* High fees
* Reputation cannot be exported

### Crypto Identity Projects

Problems:

* Identity without utility
* Difficult onboarding
* Weak incentives

## DealLock Solution

DealLock connects payments and reputation.

Every completed contract creates:

* Verified payment
* Verified review
* Verified project history

This information automatically updates a user's Live CV.

The result is a portable reputation layer that grows through real economic activity.

## Core Insight

Most work history disappears after payment.

DealLock preserves that history and converts it into trust.

## Outcome

Get paid.

Get verified.

Build reputation.
